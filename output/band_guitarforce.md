### 30.07.2016 news Guitar Force to release new album &quot;Different Universe&quot; in September.

The new album "<strong>Different Universe</strong>" is released September 16th 2016. </p><p>Guitar Force was founded in 2007 by <strong>Marcin Habaj</strong> – a guitarist, a composer and lyrics author.
