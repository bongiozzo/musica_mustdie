### 20.08.2016 news PELANDER - WITCHCRAFT main man&#39;s solo project signs with Nuclear Blast!

<p><strong>WITCHCRAFT</strong> main man, <strong>Magnus Pelander</strong>'s solo project <strong>PELANDER </strong>has officially signed to <strong>Nuclear Blast Records</strong>. </p><p>After his latest journey with WITCHCRAFT, »<strong>Nucleus</strong>«, multi instrumentalist and lyricist extraordinaire, Magnus Pelander, returns to his solo career which without a doubt can be recognized as being tied to the cult doom/rock band, still exploring other paths and going full on acoustic.</p><p>Magnus Pelander comments: <em>"At last my first solo album is done and soon to be released. I cannot believe this is true."</em></p><p>Commented Nuclear Blast A&R representative <strong>Markus Jakob</strong>: "<em>We're thrilled to not only work with a gifted artist as Magnus on his main band but now also on his solo career. Both WITCHCRAFT and PELANDER have always stood for variety, artistic freedom and development which we're more than happy to support. Prepare yourself for another deep and intense look into the mind and musical vision of a genius!</em>"</p><p>Today PELANDER also announces the as of yet untitled successor to 2010's »A Sinner's Child«-EP to be released later this year. More details about Magnus Pelander's second solo album will be revealed soon.</p><p><font color="#ffffff" style="background-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);"></font>&nbsp;<center><img width="450" height="272" src="/images/news_rus/2016.08/29836.jpg" border="0"><p></p></center>
