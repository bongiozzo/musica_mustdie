### 05.09.2016 news HOLYCIDE reveals cover art for their upcoming album

<p>Spanish thrashers<strong> HOLYCIDE</strong>, featuring <strong>Dave Rotten</strong> from <strong>AVULSED</strong> on vocals (but on a total Thrasher register here), have just revealed the cover artwork for what's going to be their first full length album that will be entitled "<strong>Annihilate... Then Ask!"</strong> whose recording phase has been finished and they're now in the mixing process. Release for the album is scheduled for January 2017 through <strong>Xtreem Music.</strong></p><p>Cover artwork for "Annihilate… Then Ask!” has been made by renowned artist <strong>Andrei Bouzikov</strong> (MUNICIPAL WASTE, TOXIC HOLOCAUST, SKELETONWITCH…) and for what you can see, it's a total Thrasher cover!!</p><p>The album will contain nine brand new songs as well as a cover version of legendary US Thrashers DЙTENTE and this is what the band said about the album: "We were in doubt between an abstract, transcendental, modern and post-whatever artwork to demonstrate we're so fucking mature, but in the end our natural born stupidity and lack of originality prevailed and since we're a Thrash band without modern bullshit, we decided to go for a cover that's total fucking 80's Thrasher as hell!! ...whattafuck!! Regarding the music, we can assure it's way superior than our previous EP, a fucking killer album!". </p><p>&nbsp;<center><img width="500" height="500" src="/images/news_rus/2016.09/29929.jpg" border="0"><p></p></center>
Автор: Vit Belov

### 04.10.2016 news HOLYCIDE launches crowdfunding campaign (pre-orders)!!

<p><strong>HOLYCIDE </strong>is finishing the mixing and mastering process of the debut full length album "<strong>Annihilate... Then Ask!</strong>" which will be a total madness for all lovers of good old 80's styled Thrash Metal.</p><p>"Annihilate... Then Ask!" contains 9 new songs plus a cover of legendary US Thrashers DЙTENTE, recorded at Blood Cadillac Studios by Andy C. who's also doing the mix & mastering. Cover artwork was done by the well known artist Andrei Bouzikov (Municipal Waste, Toxic Holocaust, Skeletonwitch...) and it's also a total 80's looking piece of art! We were thinking about a transcendental & abstract post-modern cover full of intellectual meaning behind, but... what the fuck!! A Thrash Metal album deserves a Thrash Metal artwork, haha!!</p><p>Even if the album will be released on Xtreem Music on 1st of February 2017, we are conscious that this is the underground and with such low and decreasing sales on physical formats, labels can't afford to pay studio & artwork costs that later will be difficult to recover, so we're financing everything by ourselves. For a relatively new band like us, of course it's quite some money, but we believe we're bringinig hell of a fucking awesome album and hopefully with your help by pre-ordering any of our items & combos, we'll get back, if not all, at least a good part of what we're investing in "Annihilate...
