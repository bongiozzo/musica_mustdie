### 08.06.2016 news Новый альбом OLVIDO

<p>Новый альбом экстравагантных испанцев<strong> OLVIDO "Cupido / Mors"</strong> выйдет в октября на<strong> Art Gates</strong>. Обложку нарисовал <strong>Ernest Cortada</strong> из Fosk Studios. Альбом выйдет на двух дисках - первый в жанре electronic metal, второй - gothic black metal. Продюсер альбома <strong>Alberto Seara</strong> (Sober, Hamlet, Mago de Oz, Skunk DF…).</p><p>Трек-лист:</p><p>CD 1:<br>Posando en llamas<br>Cupido Mors<br>La fin absolue du monde<br>Troyano<br>El reflejo de Werther<br>Caronte<br>Regensburg<br>Somnis entre boires (Per-version de Sangtrait)</p><p>CD 2:<br>Aedes<br>Perro negro<br>Juggernaut<br>Estadosfera<br>Soma<br>El rey de amarillo<br>Nunca me abandones<br>Vagareis por la galaxia</p><p><center><img width="600" height="600" src="/images/news_rus/2016.06/29278.jpg" border="0"><p></p></center>

### 08.06.2016 news OLVIDO: third album in October

<p>The extravagant sextet from Spain&nbsp;<strong>OLVIDO</strong> strikes back in the Metal/Rock scene with the announcement of hot details about their new record, which will be entitled "<strong>Cupido | Mors</strong>". The cover artwork has been designed by the artist <strong>Ernest Cortada</strong> from Fosk Studios.</p><p>"Cupido | Mors" will consist in a double CD that will contain an electric part with Metal elements and Gothic & Black Metal passages that characterize the extravagant sound of the band and another selection of tracks in electro-acoustic format, influenced by genres such as Dark Folk, Ethereal Wave and the musics of the world.</p><p>"Cupido | Mors" tracklist will be composed by the following tracks:</p><p>CD 1:<br>Posando en llamas<br>Cupido Mors<br>La fin absolue du monde<br>Troyano<br>El reflejo de Werther<br>Caronte<br>Regensburg<br>Somnis entre boires (Per-versiуn de Sangtraпt)</p><p>CD 2:<br>Aedes<br>Perro negro<br>Juggernaut<br>Estadosfera<br>Soma<br>El rey de amarillo<br>Nunca me abandones<br>Vagarйis por la galaxia</p><p>The band recorded “Cupido|Mors” at <strong>Musiclan Studios</strong>, produced, mixed and mastered by <strong>Alberto Seara</strong> (Sфber, Hamlet, Mago de Oz, Skunk DF…) and <strong>CUBE Studios</strong> and <strong>Joan Berenguer</strong> and Musiclan Studios.</p><p>The release is expected for October of this year. More details about this album will be unveiled soon. More info on <a href="http://www.artgatesrecords.com">www.artgatesrecords.com</a></p><p><center><img width="600" height="600" src="/images/news_rus/2016.06/29278.jpg" border="0"><p></p></center>
