### 15.10.2016 news FOREVER STILL - release new single and video clip &#39;Save Me&#39;

<p>On October 21st, the Danish high flyers<strong> FOREVER STILL</strong> unleash their debut album »<strong>Tied Down</strong>« filled with haunting hymns, like their new single <strong>'Save Me'</strong>, for which the Copenhagen based band also created a new videoclip. </p><p>"<em>Some days things just don't feel right and "Save Me" was written on one of those days", comments singer Maja Shining and explains: "When we wrote and recorded it, we wanted it to sound very stripped down and fragile, so when it was time to make the music video, it felt natural to keep that feeling in there as well. We are both huge fans of indie movies and we wanted the music video to have that kind of unedited, real life feel to it. I feel like we achieved, what we set out to do and that the video marries really well with the lyrics and music."</em></p><p><center><iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/XdptA3pwmAQ" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>
