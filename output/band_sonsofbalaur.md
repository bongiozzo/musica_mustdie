The song <strong>'Old Relics'</strong> is now streaming in clip below.</p><p>The band comments: "<em>The track 'Old Relics' deals with that religious fanatic and in his circles acclaimed vampire hunter Van Helsing. He is a relic of a forgotten time, when humans ruled the earth, when mortal men were strong enough to defend themselves from the vampires. Van Helsing is the last part of that forgotten age.
