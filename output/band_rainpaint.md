### 02.07.2002 news Rain Paint was signed to My Kingdom Records ...

<p>Rain Paint was signed to My Kingdom Records in early 2002 and is now practising and writing the last songs to the debut album "Nihil Nisi Mors"(worktitle). Although half of the songs are slow, October Tide / My Dying Bride "era". The latest additions the songs are more close to "Death" & "Opeth" in musicwise. Members of Rain Paint are known from Finnish bands such as FragileHollow, Diablerie, Rapture & Denigrate.</p>
